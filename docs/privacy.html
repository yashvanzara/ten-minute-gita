Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

10 Minute Gita ("the App"), available on iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following information on your device only:

  • Reading Progress: Which daily readings you have completed
  • Streak Data: Your current and longest reading streaks
  • Settings: Your preferences (font size, theme, notification time)
  • Reading History: Dates when you completed readings
  • Downloaded Audio: Audio files saved for offline listening

This local data is not transmitted to any server unless you create an account (see below).

Account & Sign-In (Optional)

You can optionally create an account to sync your reading progress across devices. If you sign in, we collect:

  • Name: Your display name (provided during sign-up or from your Google/Apple account)
  • Email Address: Used for account authentication
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account

Sign-in is available via Google, Apple, or email/password. We use Firebase Authentication by Google to manage accounts securely.

Cross-Device Sync

If you are signed in, your reading progress (completed readings, current day, streak data, and settings) is synced to Google Cloud Firestore so you can continue on another device. This data is tied to your account and is not shared with third parties.

Crash & Performance Reporting

The App uses Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance diagnostics. This helps us identify and fix bugs. This data includes:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Screen navigation breadcrumbs

This diagnostic data is not linked to your identity and is used solely to improve app stability.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Device advertising identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics for advertising

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for account sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore (Google) — for cross-device progress sync
  • Sentry — for crash and performance reporting

The App does not use any advertising or tracking services. None of your data is sold or shared with data brokers.

Data Storage & Deletion

Local data can be removed by uninstalling the App, using "Reset All Progress" in Settings, or clearing app data through your device settings (Android: Settings > Apps > 10 Minute Gita > Clear Data).

Account data stored in Firebase can be deleted by signing out and contacting us at the email below. We will delete your account and all associated data.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the App uses your device's local notification system. Notification preferences are stored only on your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Account creation is optional, and the App's core reading experience works without providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

You can use the App without creating an account. If you do create an account, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email above.
